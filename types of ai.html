Financial Accounting Information

Financial accounting information refers to the subset of accounting that focuses on the preparation and dissemination of financial statements and reports for external users. It involves recording, summarizing, and communicating financial transactions and events to provide a clear picture of an entity's financial health and performance.

Tax Accounting Information

Tax accounting information involves the application of accounting principles and rules to comply with tax regulations and calculate tax liabilities accurately. It focuses on recording and reporting financial information in a manner that aligns with tax laws and guidelines set by tax authorities.
